"What if my audience is too small?"

「我的受眾太少怎麼辦？」

Small audiences convert better — they're more loyal and more specific. I've seen creators with under 5,000 followers generate real revenue. Size is not the bottleneck. The system is.

小受眾的轉化率更高——他們更忠誠、更精準。我見過不足5,000追蹤者的創作者產生真實收入。受眾規模不是瓶頸，系統才是。

"I already tried selling and it didn't work."

「我已經試過銷售，但沒有效果。」

That usually means the offer wasn't built from audience data, or there was no warm-up before the pitch. Both are fixable. That's exactly what this system addresses.

這通常意味著產品沒有根據受眾數據建立，或者在推銷前沒有足夠的預熱。兩者都可以解決。這正是這套系統要解決的問題。

"How do I know this will actually work?"

「我怎麼知道這真的有效？」

You don't — and I won't pretend otherwise. That's why there's no upfront cost. The revenue share model means I only get paid when it works. My incentive is identical to yours.

你不知道——我也不會假裝你知道。這就是為什麼沒有前期費用。分潤模式意味著只有在成功的時候我才能獲得報酬。我的利益與你完全一致。

FAQ

常見問題

What if it doesn't work?

如果沒有效果怎麼辦？

Then you've lost nothing. There are no upfront fees, no retainers, no contracts that lock you in. You only pay me when revenue is generated. If nothing works, I walk away with nothing too. That's the deal.

那你什麼都沒有損失。沒有前期費用，沒有月費，沒有鎖定你的合約。只有在產生收益時你才需要付我錢。如果沒有效果，我也什麼都得不到。這就是我們的協議。

Do I lose control of my content?

我會失去對內容的控制權嗎？

No. Your content stays yours. I work behind the scenes on the funnel, email sequences, and offer — none of which touches your channel or how you create. You approve everything before it goes live.

不會。你的內容永遠屬於你。我在後台處理漏斗、電郵序列和產品——這些都不會觸及你的頻道或你的創作方式。所有內容上線前都需要你的批准。

How much of my revenue do you take?

你會抽取多少收益？

We agree on a percentage before anything is built — it's discussed on the call and written into a simple agreement. The split is always fair to both sides and depends on what I'm building and managing for you.

我們在建立任何東西之前先協議百分比——在通話中討論並寫入簡單協議。分成比例對雙方都公平，取決於我為你建立和管理的內容。

I already have a funnel. Can you still help?

我已經有漏斗了。你還能幫我嗎？

Yes — and this is often where the biggest gains are. A weak funnel with good traffic is a bigger opportunity than starting from zero. I'll audit what exists and show you exactly where it's leaking on the call.

可以——而且這往往是最大收益所在。有好流量但漏斗不強，比從零開始更有機會。我會在通話中審查現有的漏斗，並告訴你它在哪裡流失。

Why should I trust someone I've never heard of?

為什麼我要相信一個從未聽說過的人？

You shouldn't — not yet. Trust is earned, not claimed. That's why the first call costs you nothing and commits you to nothing. Come on the call, see how I think, look at the plan. Then decide. The risk is entirely on my side.

你不應該——至少現在還不應該。信任是賺來的，不是聲稱的。這就是為什麼第一次通話對你分文不收，也不要求任何承諾。來參加通話，看看我的思維方式，看看計劃。然後再決定。風險完全在我這邊。
